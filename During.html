During the Wildfire

Does and Don'ts During A Wildfire:
Does:
- Regularly check for updates on the news, social media, or radios for warnings
- Bring emergency kit and essentials if evacuation necessary
- Keep doors, windows in your home shut
- Watch for obstacles such as downed power lines and debris
- Cover home's vents, windows, and other openings with duct tape or plywood
- Position car out of the driveway with windows shut
- Turn off propane or natural gas sources and remove combustibles (move sources away from structures)
- Turn on lights in house to increase visibility in case smoke floods house

Don'ts:
- Ignore evacuation orders - Wait until the last minute to evacuate
- Stay close to the fire
- Keep windows and doors open
- Use water for non-essential tasks
- Underestimate the speed of the fire/disregard smoky conditions
- Depend on your own judgment
- Drive through a wildfire
- Return home early
- Not call for help
