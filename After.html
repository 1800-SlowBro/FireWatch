After a Wildfire

Keeping Calm
- Mentally prepare for difficult driving conditions, such as fallen trees, debris, and broken traffic lights.
- Avoid driving through areas with downed power lines and maintain a safe distance to prevent electrocution.
- Stay updated on social media for the latest information.
- Seek medical attention if required.

Returning Home
- If you were forced to evacuate your community, only return if authorities have advised it's safe to do so.
- Re-enter your home only if authorities or professional engineers have given safety clearance.
